Steven Pinker

One of my favourite writers is Steven Pinker. The Sense of Style is the thinking person’s guide to writing in the 21st century. Pinker teaches us that there is no such thing as a paragraph, no unit of discourse that consistently corresponds to a block of text. Instead, there’s the paragraph break, a visual bookmark. It’s also a powerful tool for punctuating your statements, much like you do when you pause for emphasis.
